Our Cleaning Packages

At House Hens, we offer a variety of cleaning packages tailored to meet your specific needs. Below is an informative list of our cleaning choices to help you select the one that best suits your requirements. For sample prices on these packages, please visit our Estimates page. If you desire a custom cleaning that is not listed, please contact us for your preferred choice.

General Cleaning Package

Our General Cleaning Package includes:

  • Vacuuming of exposed areas, moving items that can be lifted with one arm to clean underneath.
  • Dusting of all surfaces and window sills.
  • Cleaning of mirrors, sliding glass doors, and pictures with glass cleaner.
  • Trash removal with new liners placed in bins.
  • Bathroom cleaning: scrubbing tubs, shower doors, grout, toilets, and sinks; organizing toiletries; folding towels; washing bathroom floors.
  • Kitchen cleaning: washing countertops, exterior of appliances, inside of microwave, scrubbing sink, cleaning cabinet fronts, vacuuming, and washing floors (hot washing for tile, dry damp mopping for hardwood).

Deluxe Detail

The Deluxe Detail includes everything in the General Cleaning Package, plus:

  • Washing baseboards, doors, door frames, ceiling fans, and light fixtures.
  • Spot washing walls and removal of cobwebs.
  • Complete furniture dusting.
  • Thorough kitchen and bathroom cleaning.
  • Vacuuming and trash removal.

Delightful Detail

The Delightful Detail includes the General Cleaning Package, plus:

  • Washing baseboards.
  • Dusting doors, door frames, ceiling fans, and removal of cobwebs.
  • Complete furniture dusting.
  • Thorough kitchen and bathroom cleaning.
  • Vacuuming and trash removal.

Dusting Detail

The Dusting Detail includes the General Cleaning Package, plus:

  • Dusting of baseboards, doors, door frames, ceiling fans, and removal of cobwebs.
  • Dusting furniture.
  • Thorough kitchen cleaning.
  • Trash removal.

Carpet Shampooing

Our Carpet Shampooing service offers professional carpet cleaning to remove stains, odors, and allergens, leaving your carpets fresh and revitalized.

Window Washing

Our Window Washing service includes interior and exterior window cleaning, ensuring your windows are spotless and streak-free.
